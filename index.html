Our Why

Every Person Deserves a Chance to Shine

We believe in a world where ability is celebrated, where potential is nurtured, and where everyone has a seat at the table. At Tiki Turtle Cafe, we are not just serving food. We are serving hope, dignity, and opportunity.

When you walk through our doors, you will see something beautiful happening. Individuals with disabilities discovering their strengths, building confidence with every sandwich they craft, and realizing that their contributions matter. This is not charity. This is community. This is family. This is transformation in action.

Through hands-on culinary training, real-world experience, and an environment overflowing with aloha, our team members develop skills that extend far beyond the kitchen. They are building independence, forming friendships, and writing their own success stories.

A Program of The Arc of Hilo

The Tiki Turtle Cafe is proudly operated by The Arc of Hilo, a nonprofit organization dedicated to promoting and protecting the human rights of people with intellectual and developmental disabilities. Through meaningful employment and training opportunities, we are building a more inclusive Hawaii, one meal at a time.
