A Heartfelt Note
About Our Prices

Aloha, friends. You may notice our prices have gone up a little, and we want to be honest with you about why.

Like so many families and small kitchens across our island, we've felt the steady rise in the cost of food, fresh ingredients, and supplies. To keep serving you the quality, made-with-aloha meals you love, without cutting corners, we've had to gently adjust our prices.

But Tiki Turtle is more than a cafe. We're a nonprofit vocational training program, and every plate you order helps individuals with disabilities build real job skills, confidence, and independence.

Your meal doesn't just feed you.
It helps change a life. Every dollar stays in our mission, right here in Hilo.

Thank you for understanding, for your patience, and for choosing to spend your lunch with us. It truly means the world to our team.

Mahalo nui loa
